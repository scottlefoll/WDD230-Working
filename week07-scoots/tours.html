Scoots Tours

You have ultimate freedom when you rent a vehicle on the island. You can spend the day checking out the local history, the villages, or the beaches. You can go see some of the historic sites that are available.

Or you can book one of our tours to see the island in a different way. We have a variety of tours that will take your breathe away. You can let us do all the planning for you and just show up and enjoy the scenery.

We have the following tours available:

Cozumel Snorkeling Tour: Palancar, Columbia and El Cielo Reefs

Whether you're an experienced snorkeler or an absolute beginner, fully trained guides let you make the most of Cozumel's famous reef on this half-day snorkeling tour. Hit three of the island's hottest snorkeling spots: Palancar Reef, Columbia Reef and Playa el Cielo, known for the starfish that decorate its white sands. Your package includes drinks and snorkeling gear; marine fees are payable in cash on the day.

Price: $59 per adult
For more information, and to read reviews, please go to: Cozumel Snorkeling Tour

El Cielo Cozumel Snorkeling by Private Boat

Have fun aboard your Private boat and experience Cozumel water treasures in this incredible snorkeling adventure. This 4 hours family-friendly Private tour will take you out to the most renowned reefs and spectacular locations in the mexican coast including famous El Cielo Beach named after its white sandy bottom surrounded by beautiful starfish, definitely a true paradise on earth! Snacks, beers, soft drinks and fresh Ceviche are included and served while you enjoy the refreshing waters at your stop at El Cielito, a shallow beach area with baby rays around. This Private tour starts and ends very close to main cruise ports making it the ideal excursion for All travelers.

Price: $435 per group
For more information, and to read reviews, please go to: Cozumel Snorkeling by Private Boat

ATV Mud Kicking and Snorkeling by Boat in Cozumel

Get off road in the Cozumel jungles with this all-terrain vehicle tour. Follow your guide through the jungle to the Jade cenote, where you can take a dip and cool off. Wind up at a local beach club for some lounge time and a sailing excursion with snorkeling, lunch, and an open bar. This tour allows you to experience multiple water sports in a single day and has everything arranged for you.

Price: $89.99 per adult
For more information, and to read reviews, please go to: ATV Mud Kicking and Snorkeling
